The [Long Night of Science Jena](https://www.lndw-jena.de/en) took place on November 22, 2024, a great outreach tradition in the city. The event is open to everyone and is always a great success in bringing together the people and the science being researched in Jena.

I took part in the [Science Slam](https://www.lndw-jena.de/index.php/en/programm/science-slam), where together with other PhD students we presented our research to the general public in an understandable and fun way. It's always an honour to be able to participate in this kind of events.
